Ordinary membership application form

In order to become ad Ordinary Member you have to have obatined a certain degree of success in the field. To evaluate your achievements we have designed a point grade system: if you score is 30 points or above you're eligible to become an Ordinary Member.

All the information that you will provide below will be checked by our scientific commitee: be accurate.

Published articles

Articles in which you were involved published in journals referenced in the Index Medicus will grant you 5 points for each article

+5

SECEC / ICSS meetings attended

SECEC / ESSSE meetings and ICSS you attended (will be checked): 1 point for all authors

+5

Oral presentation

oral presentations at SECEC / ESSSE congresses or ICSS: 3 points for all authors

+5

Poster presentation

poster presentations at SECEC / ESSSE congresses or ICSS: 2 points for all authors

+5

Oral international presentation

oral presentations you made or participated in at international congresses devoted to shoulder and elbow: 2 points for all authors

+5

Book chapter

book chapters you participated in dealing with shoulder and elbow: 5 points for all authors

+5

Awards

SECEC / ESSSE awards you received

  • - Didier Patte Prize: 10 points becomes ex officio Ordinary Members
  • - Best Paper Prize: 3 points for all authors
  • - Best Poster Prize: 2 points for all authors

+5

Fellowship

SECEC fellowships: 12 points

+5

  • fellows selected to the Intra European travelling fellowship become ex officio Associate Members
  • fellows selected to the Europe / USA or Europe / Japan travelling fellowship become ex officio Ordinary Member
